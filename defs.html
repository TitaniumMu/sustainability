What does it mean?
It's important to note that 'carbon neutral' does not mean zero carbon emmissions. Carbon neutrality can technically be acheived through the purchase of 'carbon offsets'. This means that for all the carbon emitted by a company's energy usage, the company has bought an equal amount of energy from renewable sources. While this is better than nothing, offsets are the subject of some controversy, since they don't make up for the carbon being emitted, and are somewhat misleading.
